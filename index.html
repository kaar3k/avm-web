About AVM Comfort Inn

AVM Comfort Inn is ideally situated in Koramangala, one of Bengaluru's most vibrant and tech-savvy neighborhoods, making it a premier choice for both business and leisure travelers. As a leading corporate guest house in Koramangala, we offer 22 spacious Deluxe Rooms, each thoughtfully designed to provide a comfortable and relaxing stay.

Our rooms span 180 sq. ft. and feature modern decor, a comfortable bed, chair, spacious work desk, LED SMART TV, telephone, and high-speed complimentary Wi-Fi. For your convenience, each room also includes a tea/coffee maker, ironing facilities, ample power outlets, and an in-room safe for valuables, ensuring a clean hotel in Koramangala experience.
